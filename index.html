Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning service for residential properties typically includes a thorough and detailed cleaning of various areas in a home. Here are common features included in a residential deep cleaning service: General Areas: Dusting and Wiping Down Surfaces: Dust all surfaces, including shelves, baseboards, and light fixtures. Wipe down walls, door frames, and windowsills. Floor Cleaning: Vacuum carpets and rugs, including under furniture. Mop and disinfect hard floors. Windows and Mirrors: Clean all windows inside and sometimes outside. Wipe down mirrors and glass surfaces. Lighting Fixtures: Dust and clean light fixtures and ceiling fans. Kitchen: Appliances: Clean the exterior of appliances like the refrigerator, oven, and microwave. Clean and disinfect stovetops and backsplashes. Cabinets: Wipe down cabinet surfaces Countertops: Deep clean and sanitize all countertops. Sink and Faucets: Scrub sinks, disinfect, and shine faucets. Bathrooms: Toilets: Deep clean and disinfect toilets, including the exterior. Showers and Tubs: Scrub and disinfect showers, tubs, and glass doors. Sinks and Vanities: Clean and disinfect sinks and wipe down vanities. Floors: Sanitize bathroom floors. Bedrooms: Bedding: Change bed linens and pillowcases (if provided). Closets: Optional cleaning inside closets, Dusting: Dust all furniture, including dressers, nightstands, and headboards. Living Areas: Upholstery: Vacuum and clean furniture and upholstery. Bookshelves and Decor: Dust and clean bookshelves, decor items, and electronic equipment.
